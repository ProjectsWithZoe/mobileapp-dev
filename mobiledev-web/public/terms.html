Description of the Service

Humble-UI is a prompt engineering tool. It takes structured inputs from you (app idea, use case, colour palette, complexity level, and optional extra context) and generates detailed, production-grade text prompts designed to be pasted into AI code-generation tools such as Claude, GPT-4, Lovable, or Bolt to produce UI mockups.

Humble-UI does not generate UI mockups directly. It generates the prompt instructions. The quality and nature of the resulting mockup depends entirely on the third-party AI model you choose to use with the generated prompt.

The Service includes the following features depending on your plan:

  • → Prompt generation across 6 use cases (mobile app, dashboard, landing page, SaaS product, portfolio, e-commerce).
  • → 8 curated dark-mode colour palettes with exact hex values.
  • → Three complexity levels: Simple, Standard, and Advanced.
  • → Saving and loading up to your plan limit of generated prompts.
  • → One free demo generation (no account required).

We reserve the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue any aspect of the Service at any time with reasonable notice to subscribers.
